🍽️ Digital Marketing Strategy Analysis of Zomato (India)
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the digital marketing strategy of Zomato in India, focusing on how the brand builds visibility, engagement, and customer retention in a competitive food-tech market.

The study explores Zomato’s use of SEO, social media, influencer marketing, app personalization, and performance marketing to drive growth.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze Zomato’s digital marketing strategies
Understand customer engagement across platforms
Evaluate brand positioning
Compare Zomato with competitors (Swiggy)
Identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats
Suggest improvements for long-term growth

📊 Key Findings
Zomato excels in brand personality & engagement
Meme marketing drives high recall
Customers still prioritize:
Price
Delivery speed
Trust
💡 Recommendations
Improve pricing transparency
Focus on regional/vernacular content
Expand micro-influencer marketing
Strengthen trust & service reliability
Enhance personalized marketing
🧾 Conclusion

Zomato’s success lies in combining:

Performance marketing
Cultural relevance
Technology-driven personalization

To maintain leadership, it must improve trust, pricing clarity, and regional reach.
